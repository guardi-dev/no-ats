# No-ATS Manifesto

The modern hiring process is broken. 

What used to be a conversation between two professionals has turned into a bureaucratic nightmare governed by surveillance algorithms (ATS), ghost job openings, and endless, soul-crushing interview loops.

### The Problem
1. **The Algorithm Wall:** Systems filter out brilliant engineers because they don't have the right "keywords" in the right order.
2. **The Time Theft:** Companies demand 5, 7, or even 10 rounds of interviews, treating an engineer’s time as a free resource.
3. **The Ghost Market:** "Ghost jobs" stay open for months to project growth or collect data, with no intention of hiring.
4. **The Lack of Transparency:** Hidden salary ranges and vague feedback are the norm.

### Our Solution: No-ATS
No-ATS is a non-profit, open-source protocol designed to return the power to the individual.

- **Direct Connection:** No tracking, no data selling. You see a job, you email the person responsible.
- **Strict Accountability:** We don't just list jobs; we enforce standards. Companies that disrespect engineers’ time are permanently banned.
- **Open Intelligence:** We use semantic understanding, not keyword matching.
- **Trustless Environment:** The code is open. The blacklist is public. The process is transparent.

**We are not a job board. We are a filter for sanity.**
