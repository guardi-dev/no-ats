# The Rules of Engagement

To list a vacancy on No-ATS, companies must strictly adhere to the following rules. Failure to comply results in a permanent ban from the platform.

### 1. Transparency
- **Real Salary Ranges:** Every vacancy must include a clear, realistic salary range. "Competitive" or "Negotiable" is not accepted.
- **Real Openings:** Only jobs that the company intends to fill within the next 30-60 days are allowed. No "talent pooling."

### 2. The Interview Limit (The 2-Round Rule)
- **Maximum 2 Technical Stages:** After the initial screening, there can be no more than two technical evaluation rounds (e.g., one technical interview and one system design or pair programming).
- **No Free Labor:** No take-home assignments that exceed 4 hours of work. If it's longer, it must be paid.

### 3. The Feedback Loop
- **48-Hour Response:** Companies must provide a status update or feedback within 48 business hours after any interview stage.
- **The "Final Loop" Accountability:** If a company takes 5 candidates to the final interview stage and hires none of them, the company is automatically flagged for review. If no valid reason is provided, the company is **BANNED**.

### 4. Zero Data Collection
- **Direct Apply:** Companies must provide a direct email. No forced registration in external ATS systems (Workday, Greenhouse, etc.) is allowed via this platform.

### 5. Enforcement (The Blacklist)
- Any candidate can open an **Issue** to report a violation.
- Evidence (emails/screenshots) will be reviewed by the community/maintainers.
- A banned company is added to `blacklist.csv` and its vacancies are deleted. **A ban is permanent.**
